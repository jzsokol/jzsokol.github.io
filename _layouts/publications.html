EchoGPT, A Multimodal Language Model for Qualitative Reporting of Echocardiography

Conference: American Society of Echocardiography 35th Annual Scientific Sessions

Qualitative interpretation of echocardiography remains a nuanced and sophisticated discipline owing to the variety of cardiac pathophysiologies. Capturing and reproducing a full echocardiographic report with artificial intelligence (AI) in a manner that mimics a human echocardiographer remains the objective of AI researchers.

Reproduction of Full Echocardiographic Report using Machine Learning from Large-scale Echocardiogram Database

Conference: American Society of Echocardiography 34th Annual Scientific Sessions

Multiple previous studies have used machine learning tools to estimate individual measurements (e.g. ejection fraction) and pathologies (e.g. aortic sclerosis) from echocardiographic images. However, to our knowledge no study has attempted to use machine learning to recreate the full echocardiographic report.
